VISION

Our vision is to lead holistic, inclusive, innovative world class education in Nigeria.

MISSION

Our mission is to promote lifelong learning in an open and caring atmosphere that motivates students to be confident and responsible global citizens. Our Aim. Our Core Values: Our Philosophy. Our Values

Excellence

To keep pace in today's complex and competitive global arena, our teachnig methods retire old notions of educational exclusivity and embrace new models of inclusive excellence. This issue of Diversity & Democracy define how we are building pathways to success using high-impact educational practices as stepping stones toward achievement for all students

Attitude

Attitude can alter every aspect of a person's life, including their education. Student attitudes on learning determine their ability and willingness to learn. If negative attitudes are not altered. Our priority is changing students' negative attitudes towards learning thereby getting the best out of them, trust with your child and he/she will be a better student.

Discipline

Excellence can not be achieved in a vacuum, it must be driven by a disciplined ming towards set goals. Because we accept nothing less than excellence, we nurture our students on a sound displinary foundation which make them more meaningful to themselves and the society.

Discipleship

Our school program is designed with the right balance of secular education and religious mentoring that help draw our students closer to God. Through discipleship, leadership training, and missions, students are challenged and encouraged to go deep and live life with God. With us we help you grow as a leader. Embrace your identity in Christ. Go to the unreached. Discover your unique gifting. Prepare for your future.
